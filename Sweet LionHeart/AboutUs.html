Refund & Returns Policy

At Sweet LionHeart, we take pride in crafting the finest sweet treats and ensuring your complete satisfaction.

Refunds

Eligibility: Refunds are only available for orders that are incorrect on our part or not up to quality standards.

Request Process: Contact us within 24 hours with your order number and photos.

Approval: Once reviewed, approved refunds will be processed in 5–7 business days.

Returns

We do not accept returns on perishable items.

Damaged or incorrect orders must be reported within 24 hours.

Order Cancellations

48+ hours before: Eligible for full refund or store credit.

Under 48 hours: No refund.

Custom Orders

Custom and personalised cakes are non-refundable unless Sweet LionHeart is at fault.

Payment Terms

Online Orders: Full payment required to secure the order.

Deposit: R1000 non-refundable deposit required to reserve custom order dates.

Full Amount: Remainder due two weeks before the event.

Additional Policies

Products may contain allergens such as nuts, gluten, dairy, and eggs.

Improper cake handling may lead to damage that Sweet LionHeart is not responsible for.

Buttercream colour may vary due to light, temperature, and oxidation.

Refund Exceptions

In natural disasters or national restrictions, Sweet LionHeart will refund deposits when appropriate.
